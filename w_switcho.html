switch O

Switch O is a customizable switch that helps users control home appliances and access their favorite internet services. With the E-ink touch screen and removable sticker, users can fully customize the switches and place them wherever they need.

Create switch with unique interface, target and behavior on the smart phone.

8:30 Coffee ready

Use Switch O to control your smart home appliances. Such as turning all lights off before sleep, or starting the coffee machine after waking up on the bedside.

9:00 Shower and dance

Access data and functions from your favorite apps instantly. Such as playing favorite playlist from the Spotify app in the shower.

11:30 Never out of supply

Always forget to refill the salt? Buy consumables instantly right beside them when you notice the shortage.

18:00 Press for a pizza

Order food right on the fridge door, O2O services turn to buttons: just add your favorite food delivery on the switch.
