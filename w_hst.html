HAPPY SONIC TOWN

This is an education app helping kids developing the sense of sound and music. Cooperated with team from Central Conservatory of Music, we merge music theory into mini games with original tracks and sound effects.

We designed five scenes for kids to understand and build a basic sense of pitch, rhythm, and timbre. The well-designed characters and the interactions are also aimed to develop better hands-brain coordination.
