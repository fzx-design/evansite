Key Plus

Key+ is built on the PSA CEA car open platform to make it more effective for drivers to take advantages of the car functionality and enable much more possibilities. The app and its services enhance the connections between smart phone and cars. With one tap on the phone, the app will set up your car to the best status for you automatically. The idea and the demo application were awarded First Prize in PSA Car Easy Apps Global Challenge.

Scenario Automator


Program Your Car.

Scenario Automator allows users to create a series of predefined actions and activate them with just one tap. And each action can be set with different conditions. For example, user can create a scenario called “Go home” with various operations. Next time, the car will be able to complete these steps automatically with just one tap on the phone.

Endless Possibilities.

Over 20 different action patches are built in the Key+ scenario automator cover all of the most useful in-car functionalities. A powerful and user-friendly editing interface makes it simple for drivers to do full customization of their cars.

Keys & Sharing


One car for many drivers

Keys & Sharing sets the interior facilities and driving preferences, synchronize digital lifestyle data for different family members on car to customize the car sharing experiences. The system will arrange the car sharing schedule by priority when there are conflict sharing requirements. Car status (location. oil, speed) information is able to be acquired by all users linked to the car through their apps.
