Digital Chinese Brush Bundle


This bundle enables artists and amateurs to create Chinese painting and calligraphy digitally in a familiar but more advanced way which brings the classic art form to modern platforms with original inheritance and new possibilities.

PEN

Detecting brush posture and stroke form.

PAPERWEIGHT PROJECTOR

Tracking brush movements and projecting real time painting.

DIGITAL INKSTONE

Ink toning, color palette and brush wireless recharging.

Drag. Press. Sweep.

Imagine a digital stylus with the capability of writing and drawing precisely and smoothly using Chinese calligraphy techniques, this is the top on the list when I started designing user interactions. Cooperated with the product background classmates, a promising structure prototype has been made to simulate traditional brush feeling and behavior with digital capabilities.

Keep your hands clean.

A simple and elegant digital interface is designed to bring classic ink dipping and toning to a more user-friendly and sustainable form. Instead of designing a toning application, this digital ink stone makes the whole creation process much more familiar and vivid.

A wireless magnetic charging system is built underneath the cover glass. Once you put the brush pen on the ink stone, it will stay attracted and start recharging.

Digital stroke on real paper.

The HD projector built-in the paperweight keeps the feeling of brushing on Xuan paper which is crucial for Chinese painting and calligraphy. A laser scanning sensor is designed to track the movement of the brush pen and hand gesture which enables more precise detection cooperated with the pen sensors.

With the tangible interface projected on the Xuan paper, there are lots of interesting new possibilities, an interactive imitation learning template for instance. Applications like this may have the potential to engage more people becoming amateurs of this kind of art vanishing in the digital world.
