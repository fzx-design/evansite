Key Plus

2014

Award-winning smart car mobile platform

Key Plus was built on the PSA CEA car open platform to enable users to program their cars easily on the smartphone. With one tap on the phone, the app will set up your car to the best status for you automatically. The idea and the demo application were awarded First Prize in PSA Car Easy Apps Global Challenge.

A variety of action patches with user-friendly design is provided to enable fully user customization for scenarios.
