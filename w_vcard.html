VCard Weibo Client

VCard is an innovative Weibo client designed for iPad with 4 major releases. Tons of users love VCard just as we do, it was one of the best selling social apps on the App Store. VCard is my first real-world project and we are really proud of what it becomes.

Over 2 Million Downloads.

3,534 Ratings from the App Store

People love the beautiful interface and easy-to-use features of VCard. We reached 2 million downloads after releasing the 4.0 version.

VCard 5

I redesigned the interface using a content-centered concept without losing the classic VCard visual foundations. Some brand new features like PowerSearch are also added according to the feedback and use-case analyses.

VCard 4

This release brings over 50 new features and a brand new user interface with beautiful detail and animations.

Redesigned composer

A much more intuitive composer with auto-complete and smart-association @ and topic adder. I also designed a draft feature that is powerful and easy to use.

Brand new ui

VCard 4 has a reimagined user interface with refined visual language. A neat layout system is applied with tons of delicate interactive animations in order to help users understanding the interfaces.

built-in capturing and filtering

A quick and powerful photo editor is built in the capture system. User can resize, crop, and filter their photos by a few taps.

new card reader and content manager

With the new card reader, every Weibo looks like a well-organized postcard. Users can view images and videos directly by using gestures like pinch and zoom.

VCard is now open source.

Due to the policy changes of Sina Weibo Open Platform, some of the essential APIs used by VCard are no longer available for third-party developers like us. We decided to remove VCard from the App Store for it will not provide full features anymore. You may clone or download VCard source files from GitHub.
